💡 Key Discoveries
🚀
Discovery 1: Soft Pseudo Labels are the Biggest Win
Adding r5 soft pseudo labels (1176 clips × 10 oversample) from Perch teacher
as knowledge distillation targets gave the single largest improvement in the experiment series.
The jump from v9 (no pseudo) to v24 (soft KD r5) was massive.
v9 → v24: 0.7755 → 0.8141 (+0.039)
🧪
Discovery 2: Kitchen-Sink Formula Works
Combining all proven methods: ASL + dual loss + soft KD ×10 + hard pseudo ×5
+ circ_shift + strong SpecAugment yields additive improvements over individual
components. v28 (all combined) is outperforming v24 (soft KD only) at ep9.
v24 → v28: 0.8141 → 0.8053+ (still climbing)
📦
Discovery 3: Combined r4+r5 Pseudo Promising
v30 uses combined_pseudo_r4r5.csv (6601 unique clips) , 5× more
pseudo coverage than r5 alone (1176 clips). At ep2 it already hits 0.7871 —
well above the v9 baseline. Expected to exceed v24 at peak.
v30 ep2: 0.7871 (still early, ep1→2 +0.012)
❌
Discovery 4: V2S Backbone Underperforms B0 on Val AUC
Despite V2S having 22.4M params vs B0's 6.2M , all V2S variants
score below B0 on the soundscape validation set. V2S-v1=0.7012, V2S-v2=0.7113.
V2S-v3 (now running) at ep2=0.7575, still below B0 v9=0.7755.
Hypothesis: V2S needs more epochs/data to catch up, or its pretraining
features are less aligned with this val set.
Best V2S: 0.7575 vs Best B0: 0.8141 (-0.057)
🚫
Discovery 5: Rating Filter Hurts
v21 applied a rating ≥ 3 quality filter to training data,
reducing dataset size. Result: 0.7289 — worse than unfiltered v9 (0.7755).
BirdCLEF data with lower ratings still contains useful signal; aggressive
filtering removes too many examples.
v9 → v21: 0.7755 → 0.7289 (-0.047)
⚠️
Discovery 6: nPCEN (no PCEN) Hurts Significantly
v26 ablated PCEN normalization in favor of standard mel normalization.
Early stopped at ep3 with only 0.7262 — the worst B0 result.
PCEN is load-bearing for the model's ability to generalize
across soundscape recording conditions.
v9 → v26: 0.7755 → 0.7262 (-0.049) · early stop ep3
🔬
Discovery 7: Perch-Augmented Training Didn't Help
v23 used Perch outputs to augment the training data pipeline.
Early stopped at ep4 with 0.7366 — below baseline.
Hypothesis: Perch augmentation introduces domain mismatch or
the augmentation quality was insufficient. Perch is better used
as a teacher for soft labels (v24 approach) than
as a training augmenter.
v9 → v23: 0.7755 → 0.7366 (-0.039) · early stop ep4
🎯
Discovery 8: LR=5e-4 Critical for V2S
V2S-v2 used LR=3e-4 → underfitting (0.7113).
V2S-v3 corrected to LR=5e-4 → already at 0.7575 at ep2.
For larger backbones, the same LR as B0 is needed
to avoid underfitting — conservative LR scheduling is not always better.
V2S-v2 (LR=3e-4) → V2S-v3 (LR=5e-4): +0.046 at ep2
