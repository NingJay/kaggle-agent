2 Data Split History & Leak Discovery
Pre-2026-03-16 — Leaked Validation (All Experiments)
All 35,549 train_audio files were in split=train inside every embedding cache.
train_soundscapes used for both training ( use_soundscapes_in_train=True ) and validation.
Local val metrics reached 0.97–0.99 — entirely fabricated.
LB submissions (0.849 best) were the only reliable indicators.
2026-03-16 — Clean Split Reset
Stratified 80/20 split of train.csv by species (seed=42) → 7,037 holdout files stored in
configs/holdout_val_files.csv . All 8 embedding cache manifests updated:
holdout files relabelled split=holdout . CachedEmbeddingDataset already
filters split=train only, so no training code changes required.
Zero overlap between train (85,536 clips) and holdout (21,111 clips) confirmed.
2026-03-16 — Soundscape Leak Identified in nohuman-label-pseudo
Despite the train_audio holdout fix, nohuman-label-pseudo still uses
use_soundscapes_in_train=True with all 66 soundscapes in both training
and validation (no soundscape split file exists). Soundscape val score 0.97+ is inflated.
Root cause: soundscapes_split.csv not created → sc_split_csv=None
→ all soundscapes in both splits.
2026-03-16 — Fix: val_mode=holdout for Soundscape Training
New experiment nohuman-label-soundscape-train uses val_mode: holdout
— validation is the 7,037 train_audio holdout files (206 species), completely disjoint from
train_soundscapes. Soundscapes are used for training only.

8 Bottlenecks & Open Problems
Bottleneck 1 — No clean local metric until today.
All local metrics before 2026-03-16 were data-leaked and unusable.
The holdout eval for nohuman-label-pseudo (currently pending) will be the
first honest comparison against LB=0.849. Without this calibration,
every experiment decision was made blind — relying solely on LB submissions (limited to ~1/day).
Bottleneck 2 — Soundscape val still leaky in current best model.
nohuman-label-pseudo uses use_soundscapes_in_train=True without a
soundscape train/val split. The absence of soundscapes_split.csv causes all
739 soundscape clips to appear in both training and validation simultaneously.
Result: val plateau at 0.97+ is uninformative for early stopping.
Fixed in nohuman-label-soundscape-train via val_mode=holdout .
Bottleneck 3 — Domain gap between holdout and test.
Even the clean holdout (train_audio) differs from test (soundscapes) in:
label density (single vs. multi-label), acoustic conditions, and recording length.
Methods that specifically improve soundscape performance (domain adaptation,
soundscape training data) may show smaller gains on holdout than on LB.
Bottleneck 4 — SED v2 wrong hyperparameters lost a submission.
sed-b0-v2 used N_MELS=128 (should be 224), HOP=320 (should be 512),
IN_CHANS=1 (should be 3), no peak normalization → LB=0.728 vs. reference 0.862.
All 4 issues now fixed in sed-b0-v3. Wasted 1 submission + ~5h training time.
Root cause: mel parameters not validated against the reference notebook before training.
Bottleneck 5 — Non-bird species coverage.
72 non-bird species (Amphibia 35, Insecta 28, Mammalia 8, Reptilia 1) represent 31% of targets.
These species have fewer training recordings and different acoustic characteristics.
No species-aware class weighting or taxon auxiliary loss is currently active.
Could be a significant source of macro ROC-AUC loss.
